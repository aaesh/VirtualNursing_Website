HOW TAKEHELP WORKS

Takehelp provides the service of predicting diseases

Disease Predictibilty

  • From Homepage, go to the Predict Disease Section
  • you will see a Form
  • Enter the data
  • click on predict Results
  • Your Results will be shown

Search Information

  • From Homepage, go to the Search Information Section
  • you will see a searchbar
  • Enter the disease name about which you would like to search
  • click on Search
  • Your Results will be shown
